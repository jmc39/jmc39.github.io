Latitude vs. Cloudiness

In this scatterplot, latitude is along the x-axis and the percentage cloudiness is on the y-axis. For data from January 5, 2017, this plot indicates no clear pattern that establishes a relationship between latitude vs. cloudiness. This plot is literally scattered; however, for latitudes between 0 and +40, cloudiness tends to be either greater than ~80% or otherwise less than 40%. Further, the broadest distribution of percentage humidity appears at -20 latitude and also near +60 latitude.

This scatterplot graph is the third (Fig3.png) out of series of 4 graphs generated from matplotlib in python. The graph is taken from homework assignment #2 in the UCSD Extension School bootcamp "Data Science and Visualization"
